Hello everyone, I have a very interesting gist for you all. I would be glad if you would spare me a little of your time to talk about my all-in-all,the one who showed me what real love means and peace. He found me in my darkest moment when I thought no one was there for me, but He came through for me in all ways.I dont deserve such love, but He did because He is a loving Father. My heavenly Father is the gist I have. I am not sorry if it sounds lame to you, but I was once like that. When He opened my eyes, I was wowed. My all-in-all is no one but Jesus Christ Himself, the master of change. He is the one who is slow to anger and, at the same time, forgives when I realize my mistakes.
